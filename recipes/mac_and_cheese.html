Homemade mac and cheese

Mac and cheese like ya mama use to make!!!!!

Nice and thick mac and cheese that will stick to your ribs.

Eat it as a side with your favorite dish or as a main as a meatless alternative

if your into that type of thing.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain.

  2. In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to

    make a roux. Add milf to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook

    over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick. Put macaroni in

    large casserole dish,and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.

  3. Melt butter or margarine in a skillet obver medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown.

    Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika.

  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve.
